TreadLink

Treadmill-to-Garmin bridge for XIAO ESP32-S3

Flash Firmware

Connect your XIAO ESP32-S3 via USB and click the button below to flash the latest TreadLink firmware directly from your browser.

Setup Guide

1Flash the firmware using the button above
2Connect to the TreadLink WiFi network (password: treadlink)
3Open 192.168.4.1 in your browser
4Scan and connect to your treadmill
5On your Garmin watch: Settings → Sensors → Add → search for TreadLink
6Start a treadmill activity on your watch and run!

What You Need

  • Seeed Studio XIAO ESP32-S3 (with external antenna recommended)
  • A Bluetooth treadmill with FTMS support
  • A Garmin watch that supports external footpod sensors
  • USB-C cable for flashing
